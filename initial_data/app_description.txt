This application serves as a comprehensive platform for comparing and evaluating different companies across various industries. It allows users to browse company qualifications, view updated rankings of top companies, and access detailed case studies in the portfolio section. The application supports user registration and login, offering integration with Google, Microsoft, and LinkedIn for authentication. Additionally, it provides functionalities for users to create reviews, manage event resources, and update team bios, enhancing the collaborative and informational aspects of the platform. The public routes facilitate user registration, password reset, and sign-in, ensuring easy access and user management. Overall, the application is designed to assist potential customers and companies in networking, information exchange, and business growth through a structured and user-friendly online environment.
