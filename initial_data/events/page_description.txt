This page is part of the private section of a business platform designed to allow registered businesses to manage their profiles and engage with various features. Specifically, this page is dedicated to managing events associated with the business. Businesses can view their past event participations and add new events where they have been exhibitors, speakers, sponsors, organizers, or contestants. The page provides functionality to add a new event, enhancing the business's profile and visibility within the industry. This feature is crucial for businesses looking to showcase their active participation and leadership in relevant industry events, thereby boosting their credibility and networking opportunities.
